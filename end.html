Wrapping it up


Overall, it can be noted that what is consistent is that Chopin has always continuously created mazurkas throughout his composing career. From the time he started composing, the longest time stretch Chopin went without composing a mazurka is 4 years.

A mazurka is a kind of Polish folk dance with a rich cultural and historical background. Chopin moved to Paris, France early in his music career. So, this is a sign that although he was not living in his home country, Chopin is proud of his Polish identity.
